Encompass is a web-based R&D intelligence platform that identifies, ranks, and surfaces technologies relevant to operational defense needs to fill the warfighter's gaps.

Our Mission

After over 50 interviews with defense personnel, we found that there is a real disconnect between useful research in academia, and science advisors in warfare centers, who need to know that this research is available and ready for “transition”.

We are a team of undergraduate students at the University of California, San Diego, tackling this issue.
